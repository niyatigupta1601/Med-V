# 🩺 MedV

## About

MedV is a healthcare web application designed to provide quick access to a patient's essential medical information during emergencies. Patients can securely store their prescriptions, medical history, and emergency contact details, allowing authorized healthcare professionals to access critical information when timely treatment is essential.

## Features

- 🔐 Biometric authentication for secure access
- 📄 Upload and manage digital prescriptions
- 🩺 Store personal medical history
- 💊 Maintain records of medications and treatments
- 🚑 Quick access to patient information during emergencies
- 📞 Save emergency contact details
- 🔒 Secure storage of medical records
- 📱 Simple and intuitive user interface

## Problem Statement

In emergency situations, patients may be unable to communicate their medical history, allergies, or ongoing medications. This can delay treatment and increase the risk of medical errors.

MedV helps bridge this gap by making essential health information readily accessible, enabling faster and more informed medical decisions.
